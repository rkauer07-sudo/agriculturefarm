WHITEPAPER

The comprehensive guide to National Agriculture Farm — A Web3 idle farming game built on Solana

Project Overview

National Agriculture Farm is an innovative Web3 idle farming game built on the Solana blockchain. Players cultivate virtual farms, earn $AFARM tokens, and participate in a sustainable economic ecosystem designed for long-term growth and community engagement.

Our mission is to create the most beautiful and productive farming experience in Web3, combining strategic gameplay with real economic incentives. Every decision matters, from choosing crops to expanding your farm — all while contributing to a thriving token economy.

Token Economics

The $AFARM token serves as the backbone of our economic system, powering all in-game transactions, rewards, and governance mechanisms.

📊 Token Supply & Distribution:
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 $AFARM tokens
  • Circulating Supply at Launch: 200,000,000 $AFARM tokens (20%)
  • Token Allocation:
    • Game Rewards Pool: 40% (400,000,000 tokens) - Distributed gradually to players
    • Liquidity & Treasury: 25% (250,000,000 tokens) - For market stability and ecosystem growth
    • Team & Advisors: 15% (150,000,000 tokens) - Vested over 4 years with 1-year cliff
    • Community & Marketing: 10% (100,000,000 tokens) - Airdrops, partnerships, and promotions
    • Public Sale: 10% (100,000,000 tokens) - Initial token distribution
🔄 Token Utility:
  • In-Game Purchases: Buy farm plots, seeds, equipment, and upgrades
  • Staking Rewards: Stake $AFARM to earn boosted farming yields
  • Governance: Vote on game updates, new features, and treasury allocation
  • NFT Integration: Required for premium farm ownership and special events
  • Referral Bonuses: Earn additional rewards for bringing new players
💰 Economic Mechanisms:
  • Deflationary Pressure: 2% burn on all in-game transactions
  • Treasury Buybacks: Monthly repurchase of tokens using 20% of revenue
  • Staking Incentives: 5-15% APY based on lock duration
  • Reward Distribution: Daily farming rewards from the reward pool
⚠️ Important Notice: All returns and rewards in National Agriculture Farm are directly dependent on the market price of the $AFARM token. Token value fluctuations will impact earning potential, NFT values, and overall game economics. Players should understand that cryptocurrency investments carry inherent risks and should only invest what they can afford to lose.

The token's value is determined by market forces, community adoption, and the success of our ecosystem development. As the game grows and more players join, demand for $AFARM tokens naturally increases, potentially driving value appreciation.

Referral System

Our referral program is designed to reward community growth and incentivize players to bring new farmers into the ecosystem. The system operates transparently on-chain and provides sustainable income streams for active community members.

💰 Referral Rewards: Earn 10% in USDC on every shop resource purchase made by your referred players. This includes all farm expansions, crop purchases, and equipment upgrades purchased through the in-game shop.

The referral system is built on trust and transparency:

  • All referral payments are made in stable USDC currency
  • Rewards are automatically distributed when referred players make purchases
  • No minimum thresholds or complicated claiming processes
  • Referral links are permanent and track all future purchases

This system creates a virtuous cycle where experienced players benefit from bringing new participants into the ecosystem, while new players receive guidance and support from their referrers.

Treasury & Reward Pool

The project treasury serves as the foundation of our reward ecosystem, ensuring long-term sustainability and value creation for token holders.

🏦 Treasury Function: The treasury acts as a reward pool that gradually repurchases $AFARM tokens from the market. These repurchased tokens are then redistributed to players through various reward mechanisms, creating a deflationary effect and increasing token scarcity over time.

Treasury funds are sourced from:

  • Initial token sale proceeds
  • Platform fees from in-game transactions
  • Advertising revenue from partner placements
  • Strategic partnerships and sponsorships
  • Plot and resource sales - Revenue from sales of farm plots and in-game resources according to the project tokenomics, without implying direct $AFARM purchases.

The gradual repurchase mechanism ensures:

  • Controlled token supply reduction
  • Stable reward distribution to active players
  • Long-term value appreciation for token holders
  • Sustainable ecosystem growth

Advertising System

Our innovative advertising system creates a win-win situation for brands, players, and the ecosystem. Partner companies can purchase advertising space within the game environment, gaining visibility to our engaged player base while directly contributing to the reward pool.

📢 Outdoor Advertising: Partners can purchase spots on virtual billboards and outdoor signage throughout the farm environment. Their brands will be prominently displayed and integrated into the game world, creating authentic brand experiences for players.

Benefits for advertising partners:

  • Direct exposure to engaged Web3 gaming audience
  • Integration into immersive game environment
  • Positive brand association with farming and agriculture themes
  • Contribution to ecosystem sustainability
  • Measurable impact through player engagement metrics

For players, this system provides:

  • Enhanced game atmosphere with real-world brand integration
  • Additional rewards funded by advertising revenue
  • Support for continued game development and features
  • Positive perception of participating brands within the community

Brands that participate in our advertising program are viewed favorably by the farming community, creating authentic partnerships that benefit all stakeholders.

🏆 Leaderboard & Competitive Farming

National Agriculture Farm features a comprehensive leaderboard system that recognizes and rewards the most successful farmers in our community. This competitive element adds excitement and motivation to the farming experience.

🥇 Top Farmer Benefits: The highest-ranked farmers receive exclusive rewards including bonus $AFARM token allocations, rare NFT drops, and special in-game perks. These rewards are funded directly from the treasury, creating a merit-based system that benefits the most dedicated players.

The leaderboard system encourages healthy competition while rewarding players who demonstrate skill, dedication, and strategic thinking in their farming operations. Top-ranked farmers become community leaders and ambassadors for the National Agriculture Farm ecosystem.
