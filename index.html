Features

Everything On-Chain

No fake tokens, no fake balances. Every transaction is verified on the Solana blockchain in real time.

Real Blockchain Payments

Shop purchases send real USDC on-chain. You approve every transaction in Phantom before it executes.

Idle Income

$AFARM accumulates every second based on your production rate. No action required — just let it grow.

Cross-Device Progress

Farm progress is saved to the cloud and linked to your wallet. Switch devices without losing anything.

Real Token Withdrawals

Withdraw $AFARM to your wallet as real SPL tokens. The balance shows up in Phantom immediately.

Treasury Controlled Mint

The $AFARM mint authority belongs to the treasury wallet. Supply is managed and transparent on-chain.

Solana Speed

Sub-second confirmations and near-zero fees. No waiting, no gas wars — just play.
